About Me

I'm a Data Analytics Specialist at the Tennessee Department of Children's Services in Nashville. Day-to-day, I work with SQL, Excel, Power BI, and Tableau to build recurring reports for program leadership and handle ad hoc data requests from people inside and outside DCS.

Most of my work involves writing SQL queries to pull and clean data, then creating reports and dashboards. I like the challenge of taking someone's question or request and figuring out how to get them the answer they need - whether that's a quick Excel report or a full interactive dashboard.

I got into data analytics because I enjoy problem-solving and finding patterns in data. It's rewarding to do work that helps people make decisions. Outside of work, I'm always learning new techniques and tools to get better at what I do.
