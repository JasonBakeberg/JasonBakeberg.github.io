About Me

I'm a Data Analyst based in Nashville, TN, with a passion for transforming raw data into actionable insights. With expertise in SQL, Python, Tableau, Power BI, and Excel, I specialize in data cleaning, exploratory analysis, and creating interactive visualizations that drive business decisions.

My journey into data analytics has been driven by curiosity and a love for problem-solving. I enjoy uncovering patterns in complex datasets and translating technical findings into clear, impactful stories for stakeholders. Whether it's optimizing database queries, building dashboards, or automating workflows, I'm committed to delivering data solutions that make a difference.

When I'm not analyzing data, I'm continuously learning new tools and techniques to stay ahead in this ever-evolving field. Feel free to explore my projects below and connect with me on LinkedIn or GitHub!
